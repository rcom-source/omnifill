# Datex — Middle Mile Capabilities

Research date: 2026-03-31

## Company Overview

- **Product:** Datex FootPrint WMS
- **History:** 45+ years in operation, 200+ global clients
- **Target:** Mid-market 3PL, cold chain, regulated industries
- **Tech Stack:** Microsoft .NET, SQL Server, SSRS reporting, Azure-hosted SaaS
- **Microsoft Gold Partner**

[Source: https://datexcorp.com/footprint-wms/]

## Middle Mile Capabilities

### Yard Management

- Dock door appointment scheduling
- Trailer check-in and check-out
- Container tracking and trailer validation
- Real-time monitoring and optimization of inventory flow through warehouse/yard

### Cross-Docking

- Cross-docking and trans-load capabilities are core features
- Supports splitting sales orders into sub-orders fillable from multiple DCs
- Order consolidation support

### Dock Scheduling

- Dock door appointment scheduling integrated with orders, shipments, and warehouse activity
- Clients can directly schedule dock appointments via web portal
- Instant access to order statuses and related documentation

### Sortation / Routing

- References to "Network Optimization System (NOS)" and smart order routing
- Real-time data visibility for inventory positioning
- Sub-order splitting across distribution centers
