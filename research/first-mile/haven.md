# Haven (Defunct)

Research date: 2026-03-31

## Overview

Haven was a freight visibility and procurement platform founded in 2017. The company went through several pivots and shut down operations around 2022-2023. Haven's technology and team were partially absorbed by Emerge (freight procurement platform); some sources also link Haven assets to Loadsmart.

**Note:** Haven is NOT part of Optimal Dynamics (common market confusion). Optimal Dynamics is a separate company focused on AI-powered freight planning.

## Historical Capabilities

- Freight rate benchmarking and market intelligence
- Spot and contract rate management
- Carrier procurement and RFP management
- Shipment tracking and visibility
- Freight spend analytics
- Automated carrier matching

**Modes:** Primarily FTL and LTL (North American trucking). No ocean/air/rail.

## Technical Documentation (Historical)

- **REST API** for programmatic access to rate data, shipment tracking, and procurement workflows
- **Webhooks** for event notifications
- **EDI:** Basic X12 support
- **CSV/flat file** for bulk rate uploads and RFP management
- **Pre-built TMS integrations** (positioned as a layer on top of existing TMS platforms)
- **No SDKs** were ever publicly released
- **All documentation is offline** — no surviving public docs

## Authentication (Historical)

- API Key authentication
- OAuth 2.0 was reportedly on roadmap but unclear if shipped

## User Reviews (Historical)

| Pros | Cons |
|------|------|
| Strong rate benchmarking data (used DAT and proprietary data) | Company shut down — no ongoing support |
| Clean, modern UI for freight procurement | Product was narrow — mainly procurement, not full TMS |
| Good analytics for freight spend | Small carrier network vs established platforms |
| Automated carrier matching saved time | Integration capabilities were limited |
| Responsive team during operation | Frequent pivots eroded customer confidence |
| | Venture-funded with no clear path to profitability |

## Pricing (Historical)

- SaaS subscription, quote-based
- Estimated $2,000-10,000/month depending on shipment volume
- No free tier

## Current Status (2026)

Defunct. Brand, product, and APIs no longer exist. Former customers migrated to Emerge, Greenscreens.ai (rate benchmarking), or traditional TMS platforms.

## Integration Recommendation

No integration possible — company and APIs no longer exist. Remove from consideration.
