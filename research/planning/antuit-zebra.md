# Antuit.ai (Zebra Workcloud Demand Intelligence) — Supply Chain Planning Research

Research date: 2026-03-31

## Overview

Antuit.ai was acquired by Zebra Technologies and rebranded as **Zebra Workcloud Demand Intelligence Suite**. Focused on AI-driven demand forecasting, predictive ordering, lifecycle pricing, and promotion optimization for retail and CPG. [Source: https://www.zebra.com/us/en/software/workcloud-solutions/workcloud-demand-intelligence-suite.html]

**Industries:** Retail, CPG, Grocery, eCommerce

## Core Capabilities

- **Demand Forecasting:** Unified demand signal at SKU-location granularity
- **Predictive Ordering:** DSD predictive ordering and order promising
- **Lifecycle Pricing:** Markdown optimization
- **Promotion Planning:** Trade optimization
- **Allocation & Replenishment:** Omnichannel retail
- **Agentic AI:** Disruption alerts, out-of-stock/Black Swan detection, next-best-action suggestions

## ML/AI Capabilities

- Spark/PySpark distributed compute, containerized MLOps runtime
- Deep learning and ensemble methods for demand sensing
- Incorporates price elasticity, trends, seasonality, channel-specific data
- Claims accuracy for new items, slow movers, and long-tail SKUs via transfer learning
- **Data requirements:** Historical POS data, price/promotion calendars, channel data, inventory positions

## Technical Integration

- **API:** Exists at developer.zebra.com/apis but Demand Intelligence APIs are NOT publicly documented [Source: https://developer.zebra.com/apis]
- API key obtained through Zebra sales representative
- Cloud-hosted SaaS with MLOps deployment
- Data connectors to ERP/POS systems
- No public SDK

## Help Centers

- antuit.ai/resources (redirects to Zebra content)

- No dedicated community forum

## User Reviews

- **Pros:** Strong AI/ML for retail/CPG, good SKU-level accuracy, handles promotions and lifecycle pricing, Zebra ecosystem synergy (hardware + software)
- **Cons:** Very limited public documentation, enterprise-only, pricing opaque, vendor lock-in risk post-acquisition, few public reviews on G2/Capterra
- Lokad notes scope is narrower than marketing suggests [Source: https://www.lokad.com/review-of-antuit-ai/]
