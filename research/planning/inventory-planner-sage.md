# Inventory Planner (by Sage) — Supply Chain Planning Research

Research date: 2026-03-31

## Overview

Inventory Planner is a demand forecasting and replenishment tool acquired by Sage, primarily targeting eCommerce businesses (Shopify-centric). Provides automated purchase order generation, multi-location stock planning, and multi-channel inventory sync. [Source: https://apps.shopify.com/inventory-planner]

**Industries:** eCommerce, DTC, Multi-channel Retail, Shopify Merchants

## Core Capabilities

- Demand forecasting using historical sales, seasonality, lead times, supplier details
- Automated replenishment and purchase order generation
- Multi-location stock planning
- Multi-channel inventory sync
- Promotion and trend factoring

## Technical Integration

- **Developer Portal:** developer.sage.com/inventory-planner (exists but limited) [Source: https://developer.sage.com/inventory-planner]
- **Native integration:** Shopify (primary), plus other eCommerce platforms and accounting systems
- **Middleware:** Celigo, MuleSoft for ERP/eCommerce integrations
- Webhook support for event-driven integrations
- **Auth:** OAuth 2.0 (Sage platform standard)

## User Reviews

- **Pros:** Intuitive interface, accurate forecasting, streamlines purchasing decisions, unlimited users on all plans, good for multi-channel eCommerce [Source: https://www.g2.com/products/inventory-planner-by-sage/reviews]
- **Cons:** Significant API/Shopify syncing issues reported (2024-2025), stock update delays, revenue-based pricing not transparent, expensive floor price ($244.99/mo minimum)

## Pricing

- Revenue-based pricing (not fixed tiers)
- Floor price: $244.99/month
- Unlimited users on all plans
[Source: https://apps.shopify.com/inventory-planner]

## ML/AI

- Statistical forecasting: historical patterns, seasonality, trends
- No specific ML/AI model claims
- Data requirements: sales records, lead times, supplier details, promotions
